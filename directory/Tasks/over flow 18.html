Text is a unit of meaning that can be interpreted and understood. It can be written materials, such as: Books, Magazines, Newspapers, Online content. Text can also be other things, such as: Movies, Scripts, Paintings, Songs, Political cartoons, Advertisements, Maps. In literary theory, a text is any object that can be "read". This includes: Works of literature Street signs Arrangements of buildings on a city block Styles of clothing Some examples of written text include: Stories, Comic strips, Instructions, Recipes, PowerPoint presentations, Emails, Newsletters, Posters, Performances, Factual texts and explanations. Some examples of text in media studies include: TV programs, Films, Video games, Websites, Books, Songs, Podcasts, Newspaper articles, Tweets, Apps.
